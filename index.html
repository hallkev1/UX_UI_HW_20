GOT FOOD

Mobile App Design

OVERVIEW

The wide variety of meal prep delivery services out there. It is surprising that so many people have a difficult time eating nutritious, high quality meals. Through our research, we found that many of the current services miss the mark from what many of thier users actually want.

SUMMARY

This was a group project assigned through the UC Irvine UX/UI Bootcamp. As a group we decided to tackle making a mobile application to order healthy meal prep meals.

MY RESPONSIBILITIES

Within this project I took the lead role in a majority of the Research and Ideation phase. I was also partly responsible for creating some compenents that were used in the final design.

THE PROBLEM

Many people around the world struggle to eat high quality and healthy meals. Several limiting factors make healthy eating seem impossible.

WHY IS IT A PROBLEM?

1. Cooking your own meals can be time consuming.
2. Healthy ingredients are typically more expensive.
3. Fast Food is readily available and can fit into many work schedules.

OUR SOLUTION

Creating a meal prep delivery service that provides customizable, premade meals. These meals can be bought in bulk, are easy to store and transport,and only require a microwave.

WHAT WE DID.

1. Made all meals customizable and 100% ingredient transparent.
2. Ability to order in bulk to lower cost.
3. Easy to transport so busy people can bring their meals to work with them.

Research

USER INTERVIEWS

We began by conducting 6 user interviews with people between the ages of 21-40. We wanted to find out specific pain points they had with eating healthy.

WHAT WE LEARNED

After creating an affinity diagram we noticed some similarities.
1. Eating healthy is expensive.
2. Preparing meals is time consuming.

SURVEY DATA

We sent out a survey to family and friends as well as posted it on our social media. After 24 hours, we had recieved 60 responses..

OUR FINDINGS

We noticed a trend between our User interviews and Survey data. We were able to confidenly argue that the one of the reasons people had a hard time eating healthy is due to time and money.

Definition and Ideation

WHO ARE OUR USERS?

Our users are everyday working people who want to start eating healthy. Their goals are to find affordable meals that they can take on the go. They have an extremely busy schedule and don’t want to cook during their valued time off.

I LIKE, I WISH, WHAT IF

After having a stronger understanding of who our users really are, we started brainstorming through using the I like, I wish, What if method.

Feature Prioritization Matrix

WIREFRAMES

LOW FIDELITY WIREFRAMES

As a group we decided to each create our own set of Low-Fi wireframes. After creating our own versions we did testing to find out what our users liked and disliked.

HIGH FIDELITY WIREFRAMES

After doing some indivdual testing on our Low-Fi designs we worked as a team to create one set of Hi-Fi designs. These designs took into account the positives and negatives from the previous testing data.

ONBOARDING

We know that you dont have time to spare. Through our onboarding process we find out about you and only show you meals that we think you will like.

CREATING YOUR OWN MEALS

Want some thing more specific? We make creating your own meal preps simple. You simply choose 3 options and we do the work for you.

NEXT STEPS

If we were asked to continue updating this project we have a few things in mind we would like to work on. As a team we would like to add more options for people with highly specific diets.
